10 Underrated AI Tools You Didn’t Know Existed for Productivity

In the fast-paced world of technology, AI tools are transforming how we work, learn, and manage our time. While everyone talks about ChatGPT and MidJourney, there are countless underrated AI tools that can supercharge your productivity. Here are 10 hidden gems you should start using today.

1. Notion AI

What it does: Enhances your note-taking and project management with AI-powered suggestions and automation.

Why it’s great: It integrates seamlessly with Notion’s existing features, making it perfect for teams and individuals.

Example: Use Notion AI to automatically summarize meeting notes, generate to-do lists, or even draft blog posts. For instance, after a brainstorming session, you can ask Notion AI to create a structured outline for your project.

2. Otter.ai

What it does: Transcribes meetings, interviews, and lectures in real-time with high accuracy.

Why it’s great: Saves hours of manual transcription and helps you focus on the conversation.

Example: During a client call, Otter.ai can transcribe the entire conversation, highlight action items, and even identify speakers. You can later search for specific keywords in the transcript to find important details.

3. Taskade

What it does: Combines task management, mind mapping, and AI-driven workflows in one platform.

Why it’s great: Perfect for remote teams and solo entrepreneurs looking to streamline their workflows.

Example: Create a mind map for a new marketing campaign, and Taskade’s AI will suggest tasks, deadlines, and dependencies. It can also automate repetitive tasks like sending reminders.

4. Fireflies.ai

What it does: Records, transcribes, and analyzes meetings automatically.

Why it’s great: It integrates with tools like Zoom and Slack, making it a must-have for remote teams.

Example: After a team meeting, Fireflies.ai can generate a summary with key points, action items, and even sentiment analysis. You can share this summary with absent team members instantly.

5. Mem.ai

What it does: Organizes your notes and ideas using AI to surface relevant information when you need it.

Why it’s great: It’s like having a second brain that remembers everything for you.

Example: If you’re working on a presentation and need stats from a meeting six months ago, Mem.ai can instantly retrieve that information for you, saving you hours of searching.

6. SaneBox

What it does: Uses AI to filter and prioritize your emails, saving you hours every week.

Why it’s great: It works with any email provider and learns your preferences over time.

Example: SaneBox can automatically move low-priority emails (like newsletters) to a separate folder, so you only see important emails in your inbox. It can also remind you to follow up on emails you’ve ignored.

7. Clockwise

What it does: Optimizes your calendar to create more focus time and reduce meetings.

Why it’s great: It’s perfect for professionals juggling multiple commitments.

Example: Clockwise can automatically reschedule meetings to create blocks of uninterrupted focus time. For instance, if you have back-to-back meetings, it will move one to a later time to give you a break.

8. Trevor AI

What it does: Helps you schedule tasks and manage your time more effectively.

Why it’s great: It’s simple, intuitive, and integrates with your existing tools.

Example: If you have a long to-do list, Trevor AI can help you prioritize tasks and allocate time slots for each. For example, it can schedule 2 hours for writing a report and 1 hour for responding to emails.

9. Wordtune

What it does: Rewrites and improves your writing in real-time.

Why it’s great: It’s a lifesaver for anyone who writes emails, reports, or content regularly.

Example: If you’re struggling to phrase an email, Wordtune can suggest multiple versions, from formal to casual tones. For instance, it can turn “I need this by tomorrow” into “Could you please prioritize this and share it by tomorrow?”

10. Motion

What it does: Automates your daily schedule and task management using AI.

Why it’s great: It adapts to your priorities and helps you stay on track effortlessly.

Example: If an urgent task comes up, Motion can reschedule your less important tasks automatically. For instance, if a client requests a last-minute meeting, Motion will adjust your calendar to accommodate it without disrupting your workflow.

Final Thoughts

These underrated AI tools are proof that you don’t need to rely on the most popular apps to boost your productivity. Whether you’re a student, professional, or entrepreneur, these tools can help you work smarter, not harder. Give them a try and see how they transform your workflow!
